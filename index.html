About WebStart Hub

WebStart Hub is a free educational platform that teaches absolute beginners how to build websites using modern HTML5 techniques. We focus on writing clean, semantic code that works well for both people and search engines.

Why Accessibility Matters

Accessible websites ensure that everyone—including users with disabilities—can navigate and interact with online content. At WebStart Hub, we teach the importance of semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, and screen reader-friendly design.

For example, we use semantic tags like <nav> and <footer>, and apply attributes like aria-label for better screen reader support.

Getting Started with SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) helps your content be found by people through search engines like Google. A well-structured HTML page with proper headings, meta tags, and meaningful content is essential for good SEO.

We emphasize the use of descriptive , logical heading hierarchy, and meaningful text to make your pages both human-friendly and search-engine friendly.
